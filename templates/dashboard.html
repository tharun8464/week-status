Help & Guidelines

Report Format
  • Include your name and the reporting period
  • Summarize tasks completed during the week
  • List any challenges encountered
  • Outline your plan for the upcoming week
  • PDF format is preferred, but other formats are accepted
Submission Tips
  • Submit reports by Sunday at 11:59 PM
  • Name your file clearly with the date included
  • Keep your report concise but comprehensive
  • Include relevant metrics and achievements
  • Use the Excel template for consistent formatting
  • Contact support if you encounter any issues
